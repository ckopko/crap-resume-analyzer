Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: QuantumLeap Analytics

We are seeking a motivated Data Analyst to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for data and a knack for solving complex problems. You will be responsible for turning data into actionable insights that will help guide our business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop business intelligence solutions using Tableau or similar BI tools.
- Write complex SQL queries, including Common Table Expressions (CTEs) and window functions, to extract and manipulate data from our data warehouse.
- Use Python to perform statistical analysis and build predictive models.
- Communicate findings to stakeholders through clear and concise reports and presentations.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand data needs and deliver solutions.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field like Statistics, Mathematics, or Computer Science.
- 2+ years of experience in a data analyst role.
- Expert proficiency in SQL.
- Strong experience with a data visualization tool, preferably Tableau.
- Proficiency in Python for data analysis (Pandas, NumPy).
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.
